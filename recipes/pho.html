This is pho

One of the best pho in HCMC. It is also the famous engineer Tung favorite pho. It tastes good, and expensive.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Pho noodles
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Go to 415 Nguyen Trai, 5th District, HCMC
  2. Get to your table, tell the server "Cho 1 to pho bo tai"
  3. Wait about 3-5 minutes to get your bow of pho
  4. Eat
  5. Pay (optional)
